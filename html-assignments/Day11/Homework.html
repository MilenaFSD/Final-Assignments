From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden.

Often flowers (edible and non-edible)and herbs are planted with wegetables to enhanchethe garden beauty

The goalis to make the function of providing food aesthetically plasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form...

Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance

Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
